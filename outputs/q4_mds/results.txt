Why stress decreases with increasing dimensions:
------------------------------------------------------------
1. More degrees of freedom allow better distance preservation
2. Higher dimensions reduce constraints on point positioning
3. At original dimensionality, perfect embedding is possible
4. Stress = 0 means all pairwise distances are perfectly preserved
