Q5 Part 1: Manifold Learning Comparison - DIGITS
======================================================================

Dataset: digits
Samples: 1797
Features: 64
Classes: 10

Computation Times:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
t-SNE          :     4.64s
LLE            :     0.18s
UMAP           :    10.68s
Isomap         :     0.51s


Method Characteristics:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
t-SNE:
  - Preserves local structure very well
  - Good for visualization and cluster discovery
  - Non-deterministic (different runs give different results)
  - Slow for large datasets

LLE (Locally Linear Embedding):
  - Assumes data lies on locally linear patches
  - Preserves local neighborhood structure
  - Faster than t-SNE
  - Can struggle with noisy data

UMAP:
  - Preserves both local and global structure
  - Faster than t-SNE
  - Better scalability to large datasets
  - Often produces tighter clusters than t-SNE

Isomap:
  - Preserves geodesic distances
  - Good for datasets on curved manifolds
  - Sensitive to neighborhood size parameter
  - Can handle non-linear structures
